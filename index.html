Project Background

The auto industry is looking for ways to advance the auto industry specifically by targetting driver convienience and safety. Lane managemenment systems do exactly that. These systems ensure the safety of the road overall by ensuring that drivers are within the bounds of the lane whenever possible.

Project Description

We are creating a Lane Management System (LMS) that incorporates 3 industry standard subsystems:

Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS) - Designed to alert drivers when they drift out of their lane.

Lane Centering System (LCS) - Designed to track the vehicle's position within the lane.

Lane Keeping System (LKS) - Applies adjustments to the vehicle in order to maintain its position within the bounds of the lane.
